The multi-location challenge

Growing from 1 office to 5, 10, or 15+ creates blind spots you can't afford in healthcare.

Regulatory surprises

OSHA and state licensing inspections catch issues you didn't know existed. Average fine: $3,000–$15,000.

Patient reviews suffer

"Dirty waiting room" or "outdated office" reviews cost ~$200+ per lost patient in lifetime value.

Owner bandwidth

Visiting 10 locations × 12 times/year × 4 hours = 480 hours. That's 12 full work weeks driving between offices.

Paper checklists fail

No photos, no scores, no trending, no accountability. Paper gets filed and forgotten.

No objective data

Without scored evaluations, you can't compare locations, identify patterns, or hold managers accountable.

Inconsistent patient experience

Exam room supplies, equipment readiness, optical merchandising, and front desk protocols vary wildly between locations.

Standardized checks your staff can actually follow
